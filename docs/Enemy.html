Enemy(options)

The enemy of game An enemy has a name, HP, attack power, and abilities

Constructor

new Enemy(options)

Creates a new Enemy instance.* This constructor initializes the enemy's name, current HP, maximum HP, and image. It also sets up the Shadow DOM to visually display the enemy's status.
